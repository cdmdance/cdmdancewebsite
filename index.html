About Us

Your Tampa Bay Dance Journey Starts Here.

At CDM Dance, our mission is simple: to inspire confidence, connection, and joy through the art of ballroom and Latin dance. We serve the entire Tampa Bay region, bringing professional instruction directly to you — in your home or in-studio.

Whether you're a complete beginner stepping onto the dance floor for the very first time, a couple preparing for your wedding dance, or someone looking to refine your technique, every lesson is tailored to your specific goals, pace, and personality.

We believe dance should be accessible, fun, and deeply rewarding. That's why we offer flexible scheduling, in-home lessons, and a relaxed, encouraging environment where every student thrives.
